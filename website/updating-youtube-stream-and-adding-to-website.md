# Updating YouTube Stream and Adding to Website
1. Open YouTube Studio
1. Navigate to video in Live tab
1. Click video to edit
1. Title should be the reference or title if topical series followed by a bar and speaker
  - Example: Genesis 1 \| Gary Campbell Jr.
1. Description should be:<br><code>You can find a sermon outline and small group questions on our website here - https://www.grotonbiblechapel.org/sermon-index<br><br>Are you new to GBC? Click here to connect with us and learn more: https://www.grotonbiblechapel.org/im-new<br><br>Connect With Us<br>Visit our website: http://grotonbiblechapel.org<br>Instagram: http://instagram.com/grotonbiblechapel<br>Facebook: http://facebook.com/grotonbiblechapel</code>
1. Thumbnail should be uploaded to the thumbnail section
1. Playlist should be for the message series
  - Also include any additional playlists as well such as baptism or child dedication, sometimes a video will be in multiple playlists
1. Check the box saying No, it is not made for kids
1. Click Save
1. Copy the link underneath the YouTube video on the top right
1. Open Squarespace
1. Navigate to Sermons blog and correct item and click Edit
1. Click on the video element and choose edit
1. Click Add Video, then Add From Link
1. Paste the link copied above
1. Click Add
1. Save Page and Exit
