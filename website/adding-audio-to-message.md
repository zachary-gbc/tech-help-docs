# Adding Audio to Message
1.	Go To YouTube Studio ([Click To Open](https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC28m_3V-lmLMuxPqQSACjYQ/videos))
  - Click Content > Live if needed
  - Uploaded videos are in Video tab, and Live videos are in Live tab
1. Click on video to be downloaded
1. Copy link to video (on right under picture of video)
1. Open ClipGrab
1. Paste link to video
1. Download as mp3 (audio only)
1. Open Squarespace
1. Navigate to Sermons blog and correct item and click Edit
1. Under YouTube Video click the plus button and choose Audio
1. Under Embed tab:
  - Upload audio that was downloaded in step 6
  - For Title use format: <reference> | Sermon Series: <sermon series>
    - Example: Genesis 1 \| Sermon Series: Starting with the Beginning
  - For Author / Artist use format: <speaker> - Groton Bible Chapel
    - Example: Gary Campbell Jr. - Groton Bible Chapel
1. Under Design Tab check box to Show Download Link
